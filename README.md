### About Me 👻
---
I'm a **Systems Engineer** (graduated from Universidad Distrital F. J. C.) with a deep passion for the entire software lifecycle, from low-level systems (my Linux roots!) to high-level cloud architecture.

* 💼 Currently applying my skills as a **Systems Analyst** in the **FinTech** sector, focusing on microservices and IaC.
* ☁️ My core stack includes **Java (Spring Boot, Quarkus)**, **Angular**, **Python**, **AWS**, **Terraform**, **Docker**, and **CI/CD**.
* 🎓 My 5.0-graded thesis explored AI, steganography, and chaotic attractors.
* 🌱 I'm a firm believer in open-source and was the former Director of the **[@GLUD](github.com/glud)** (GNU/Linux Group) and a member of the **ComplexUD** and **GICOGE** research groups.

My goal is to design, build, and maintain scalable, secure, and efficient solutions.
